📝 Understanding Type 1 Diabetes

Living Well With Type 1

Type 1 Diabetes is a chronic condition in which the pancreas produces little or no insulin. Managing it well means checking your blood sugar levels regularly, maintaining a healthy diet, and staying active. Remember, you're not alone—many people live vibrant, full lives with Type 1!

Support systems, like Nurse Betty, can help remind you to check levels, understand trends, and even provide emotional encouragement. Be kind to yourself—you are doing something remarkable each day. 💙
