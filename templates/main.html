💙 A Letter From Mom – Why This Site Exists

My sweet, caring, funny son was in his late thirties and embarking on a new future,
studying hard at UC Berkeley, when out of nowhere he found himself in the worst nightmare —
plus two shades darker.

Type 1 Diabetes.

The medical world basically hands you medicine, a few pamphlets,
and sends you home to live a life you have no idea what's in store for you.

You bounce around, doing your best, taking your shot twice a day,
changing your diet, thinking you're going to be somewhat okay.

Then one day you wake up in a hospital, looking at people you don't know,
who are telling you that you have been in a coma for the last three days —
and you are very lucky to be alive.

As it turns out, a headache and throwing up means you're in big trouble.

My hope for this site is that it will help him and others.
Sharing your experiences. Learning how to live your best life.

Sadly, this is not going to be easy.
But as this site grows, we will have an open chat to share experiences.

Love,
Mom
